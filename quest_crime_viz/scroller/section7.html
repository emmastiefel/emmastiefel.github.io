Where do Minerva students say they feel safe?

The short answer to this question is: Seoul. Most students who responded to the survey had lived in the Yongsan neighborhood residence, which Minerva has used to house students from the class of 2021 on, but the same trend applied to the Gangnam neighborhood residence as well.
