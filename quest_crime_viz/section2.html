How does 16 Turk compare to other Minerva residence halls in San Francisco?

The rate of crime reported near 16 Turk is over 3 times higher than the 2019 average daily reports for both the 851 California Street and 1412 Market Street residence halls. Compared to the approximately 15.9 crimes reported each day near 16 Turk, there were approximately 3.3 reported near 851 California Street and 4.3 reported near 1412 Market Street.
