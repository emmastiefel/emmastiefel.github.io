What crimes have students reported near each residence hall?

Of the 56 students who responded to Quest’s survey, approximately 17 reported that they had been the victim of a crime or disturbing harassment near a Minerva residence hall. Most of these students (9 out of 17) said that the crime or harassment had occurred near 16 Turk Street, and 5 out of 17 said the crime or harassment had occurred near 1412 Market Street.
