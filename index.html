Hi, I’m Emma Stiefel.

I’m a journalist who uses data and extensive research to explain complex issues. I know data analysis is a powerful tool, but I also believe in emphasizing what the numbers can’t show us. I strive to meet my readers where they’re at in terms of data literacy and background knowledge.

Currently, I’m a technical contractor for Metrics for News and editor-in-chief of the Minerva Quest, my college news publication. In the past, I’ve been an editorial intern at Cincinnati CityBeat, a reporting intern at the Hamilton Journal-News, a tech support intern for Minerva Project, and a content development intern at Minerva Schools at KGI. I was also selected for WSJ Noted.’s advisers program and AAJA’s Voices fellowship. I’m a senior at Minerva Schools at KGI, where I am majoring in data science and history with a focus on digital humanities.
