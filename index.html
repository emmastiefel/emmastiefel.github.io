Hi, I’m Emma Stiefel.

I use data, design, and storytelling to explain complex systems with an emphasis on what we don’t know about a topic as well as what we do. I strive to meet my audience where they’re at in terms of data literacy and background knowledge and make sure that my work serves the information needs of a community.

Currently, I’m a technical contractor for Metrics for News and editor-in-chief of the Minerva Quest, my college news publication. In the past, I’ve been an editorial intern at Cincinnati CityBeat, a tech support intern for Minerva Project, a content development intern at Minerva Schools at KGI, and a reporting intern at the Hamilton Journal-News. I’ve also participated in WSJ Noted.’s advisers program and AAJA’s Voices fellowship.
